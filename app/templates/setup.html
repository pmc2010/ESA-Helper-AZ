Welcome to ESA Helper

ESA Helper automates your ClassWallet reimbursement submissions, saving you 10+ minutes per request.

This setup wizard will help you configure your ESA account and set up your family's student profiles. You can manage and update these settings anytime from the Settings menu.

System Requirements
Your system meets these requirements
Operating System: macOS, Windows, or Linux ✓ Supported
Python: 3.11 or higher ✓ Installed
Web Browser: Chrome/Chromium (for automation) ✓ Required
LibreOffice: For PDF conversion (optional) ⚠ Optional

Note: See the Requirements page for complete installation instructions.

Step 1 ESA Account Credentials

Enter your ClassWallet ESA account credentials. These will be securely stored locally and used to automate form submissions.

The email address you use to log into ClassWallet.
Your ClassWallet password. Stored locally only, never sent to third parties.
Security Note: Your credentials are stored in config.json on your computer. Never share this file with others. The automation requires credentials to log in and submit forms on your behalf.
Step 2 Add Your Students

Add each of your children as a student profile. You'll need at least one to use ESA Helper.

Already Added: The following default students are available as examples:
  • Student One
  • Student Two
  • Student Three
You can edit these or add new students in the Settings menu.
